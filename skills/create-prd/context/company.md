# Company Context — VisitLeMarche.com

## Overview

VisitLeMarche.com is an independent, multilingual travel website focused on promoting the Marche region in Italy to an international audience.

The platform combines:
- High-quality editorial travel content
- Practical planning tools (transport, accommodation, itineraries)
- Affiliate integrations for monetisation

The goal is to position Le Marche as a credible alternative to more saturated Italian destinations (e.g. Tuscany, Amalfi Coast), highlighting its authenticity, accessibility, and affordability.

---

## Mission

Make Le Marche easier to discover, understand, and travel to for international users.

---

## Vision

Become the reference platform for:
- Planning a trip to Le Marche
- Discovering lesser-known destinations in Italy
- Combining content + booking in a single experience

---

## Target Audience

### Primary
- International travellers (UK, Germany, Netherlands, France)
- Age: 25–55
- Interests:
  - Slow travel
  - Nature, beaches, hiking
  - Authentic Italian experiences
  - Affordable alternatives to mainstream destinations

### Secondary
- Italian travellers exploring other regions
- Niche audiences (hiking, cycling, cultural tourism)

---

## Core Value Proposition

- Curated, structured, practical travel guides (not generic blogs)
- Strong focus on logistics:
  - How to get there
  - Where to stay
  - What to expect
- Multilingual accessibility
- Integration of booking tools directly within content

---

## Business Model

Affiliate-driven:
- Flights (e.g. TravelPayouts, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Accommodation (e.g. Booking, Vio, aggregators)
- Activities (e.g. GetYourGuide)

Future potential:
- Bundled offers (flight + hotel)
- Featured listings / partnerships
- Custom itineraries

---

## Strategic Priorities

1. **SEO & Organic Growth**
   - Target high-intent keywords (e.g. “beaches in Le Marche”, “how to get to Ancona”)
   - Build topical authority

2. **Conversion Optimisation**
   - Embed booking options directly in guides
   - Reduce friction between inspiration → action

3. **Content Scalability**
   - Use AI-assisted workflows
   - Maintain quality while scaling multilingual content

4. **User Experience**
   - Fast, mobile-first
   - Clear structure (maps, sections, practical info)

---

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct competitors
- Generic travel blogs (low structure, low reliability)
- National tourism websites (institutional, less practical)

### Indirect competitors
- Booking platforms (Booking, Expedia)
- Aggregators (Kayak, Skyscanner)

### Differentiation
- Depth + structure of content
- Region-specific focus
- Integration of planning + booking

---

## Key Constraints

- Solo operation (limited bandwidth)
- Heavy reliance on SEO traffic
- Affiliate attribution limitations (cookies, redirects)

---

## Success Metrics

- Organic traffic growth
- Conversion rate (click → booking)
- Revenue per page
- Engagement (time on page, bounce rate)

---

## Product Philosophy

- Practical over inspirational
- Clear over verbose
- Structured over narrative
- Always actionable

---

## Notes for AI Agents

When generating features or PRDs:
- Prioritise SEO + conversion impact
- Avoid generic travel content
- Always include:
  - logistics
  - structure
  - monetisation opportunity
